01

Vision

Design an innovative platform that bridges the physical and digital worlds, allowing users to remotely control real-world drones and explore cities across the globe in real time.

The platform lets users connect via the internet to live drone feeds deployed in major cities — not for passive viewing, but for active exploration and real-time interaction. The goal is to bring a new form of presence to the digital experience.

"Not passive viewing — active exploration and interaction."
02

03

Infrastructure

The system is supported by 4 different drone models, engineered for real-time control, low latency, and stable connectivity. The infrastructure prioritizes:

⚡
Real-Time Control
Direct user input with sub-200ms latency targets for seamless interactive navigation.
📡
Low Latency Streaming
Live 4K video feeds optimized for web delivery across all modern devices and connection types.
🔒
Stable Connectivity
Redundant network layers ensure uninterrupted sessions with automatic failover protocols.
04

Gamification

In later stages, the platform will introduce mission-based mechanics and gamification. Users will be able to:

01
Location Challenges
Complete location-based tasks and navigation challenges across all 8 cities.
02
Earn Rewards
Earn rewards through interactive exploration experiences and milestone completions.
03
Compete Globally
Compete in community leaderboards and ranked missions against pilots worldwide.
04
Exclusive Access
Unlock exclusive city access and rare drone models through sustained engagement.
◈ Engagement is not passive — it is earned.
