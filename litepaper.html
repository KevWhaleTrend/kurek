Project Vision

VOIDYACHT is an advanced engineering and AI-supported, blockchain-based racing and reward ecosystem consisting of 9 different fully autonomous (unmanned) racing boats. It offers users a technological and economic experience by combining real-world autonomous system logic with Web3 infrastructure.

Our vision is to integrate autonomous marine vehicle technology with Web3, creating an ecosystem where users don't just invest, but also experience, analyze, and interact.

Not just an NFT project, but a hybrid platform where autonomous system simulation, competition, and an economic model converge.

03

How it Works?

VoidYacht is not just about racing. It is a unique journey of engineering and intelligence where digital ownership rules the real waters.

Step 01

Mint Now

Mint any (or more than one) of the 9 unique autonomous racing boats as an NFT. Each boat is designed with different AI software, sensor sets, and racing strategies. At this stage, you can carefully examine the features while choosing your boat.

Step 02

Subscribe

You pay a monthly subscription fee to keep your minted boat ready for races. Thanks to the subscription, your boat's AI software is regularly updated, is maintained, and can participate in races with full performance every week.

Step 03

Race Mode

Every week on a designated day, 9 boats start racing fully unmanned and autonomously. The boats plan their routes in real-time with their advanced AI, analyzing wind and wave conditions. The entire race is managed unmanned.

Step 04

Treasure

When the race is completed, the NFT owners of the winning boat share the weekly treasure pool proportionally. You receive a reward share equal to the number of NFTs you own. The more NFTs you have, the more your potential earnings increase.

Utility and Economic Model

The VOIDYACHT ecosystem offers more than simple ownership—it enables active participation.

Each NFT holder gains:

  • • Access to participate in weekly autonomous races
  • • Proportional share of the treasury pool if their yacht wins
  • • Ability to keep their yacht active through a monthly subscription
  • • Access to real-time telemetry and race data
  • • Performance tracking through advanced analytics tools
  • • Entry to the alpha community and early feature updates

Additionally:

  • • Data transparency is a core principle
  • • All race results are presented in a verifiable manner
  • • Long-term features such as staking, upgrades, and modular enhancements are planned
