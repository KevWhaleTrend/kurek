How it Works?

VoidYacht is not just about racing. It is a unique journey of engineering and intelligence where digital ownership rules the real waters. Turn the pages and discover the privileged path that autonomous technology offers you.

01 / MINT

Mint Now

Mint any (or more than one) of the 9 unique autonomous racing boats as an NFT. Each boat is designed with different AI software, sensor sets, and racing strategies. At this stage, you can carefully examine the features while choosing your boat.

02 / SUBSCRIPTION

Subscribe Monthly

You pay a monthly subscription fee to keep your minted boat ready for races. Thanks to the subscription, your boat's AI software is regularly updated, is maintained, and can participate in races with full performance every week.

03 / RACING

Weekly Autonomous Race

Every week on a designated day, 9 boats start racing fully unmanned and autonomously. The boats plan their routes in real-time with their advanced AI, analyzing wind and wave conditions. The entire race is managed unmanned.

04 / EARNINGS

Share the Treasure

When the race is completed, the NFT owners of the winning boat share the weekly treasure pool proportionally. You receive a reward share equal to the number of NFTs you own. The more NFTs you have, the more your potential earnings increase.
